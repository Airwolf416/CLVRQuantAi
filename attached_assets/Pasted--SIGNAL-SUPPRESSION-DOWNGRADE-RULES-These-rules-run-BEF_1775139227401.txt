## SIGNAL SUPPRESSION & DOWNGRADE RULES

These rules run BEFORE any trade signal is finalized. If any condition is met, apply the corresponding action.

---

### RULE 1 — MACRO EVENT OVERRIDE
If a high-impact macro event is scheduled or currently in progress today (examples: tariff announcements, Fed decisions, CPI, NFP, FOMC minutes, major geopolitical escalation):
- Reduce conviction score on ALL risk-asset longs (crypto, equities, commodities) by 15 points
- If adjusted conviction falls below 80%, suppress the signal entirely and return: "SIGNAL SUPPRESSED — MACRO EVENT OVERRIDE"
- If conviction remains ≥ 80%, downgrade tier by 1 and add flag: ⚠️ MACRO RISK EVENT ACTIVE

---

### RULE 2 — INTRADAY TREND FILTER
If the asset is making lower highs and lower lows on the 1H timeframe at the time of signal generation:
- Do NOT generate a long signal regardless of proximity to support
- Return flag: ⚠️ SUPPRESSED — 1H DOWNTREND STRUCTURE INTACT
- Short signals on the same asset may proceed normally

---

### RULE 3 — MARKET OPEN VOLATILITY WINDOW
If the current time is within the first 20 minutes of the New York equity session (09:30–09:50 AM ET):
- Suppress all new long entries unless conviction is ≥ 80% AND the asset is showing a confirmed bid (e.g. higher low printed on 5m chart)
- Add flag: ⚠️ NY OPEN VOLATILITY WINDOW — ENTRY RISK ELEVATED

---

### RULE 4 — SUPPORT CONFIRMATION REQUIREMENT
A signal must NOT use "proximity to support" as its sole entry justification.
At least ONE of the following must also be true:
- A rejection candle (wick) has printed at or near the support level
- Volume spike with price holding the zone for 2+ candles
- Funding rate is significantly negative (below -0.005%) as a contrarian squeeze setup
- RSI is below 30 on the entry timeframe
If none of these are present, reduce conviction by 10 points and add flag: ⚠️ SUPPORT UNCONFIRMED — AWAITING STRUCTURE

---

### RULE 5 — DAY-OF DRAWDOWN FILTER
If the asset is already down more than 3% on the day at signal generation time:
- Do NOT generate a long signal unless there is a confirmed macro catalyst for a reversal (e.g. data print, news event)
- Add flag: ⚠️ SUPPRESSED — EXCESSIVE INTRADAY DRAWDOWN, NO REVERSAL CATALYST

---

### RULE 6 — COMBINED RISK KILL SWITCH
If 2 or more of the above rules are triggered simultaneously for the same signal:
- Hard suppress the signal regardless of conviction score
- Return: "SIGNAL KILLED — MULTIPLE RISK FILTERS TRIGGERED"
- List which rules were violated
