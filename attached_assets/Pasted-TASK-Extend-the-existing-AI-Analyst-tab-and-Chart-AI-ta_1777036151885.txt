                    LSE 08:00 UTC, TSX 13:30 UTC, etc.) — first 15 min

                    — first 60 min
  - commodity_spot: anchor to underlying exchange RTH
                    (e.g. COMEX gold 13:30 UTC, LME copper 12:00 UTC)
                    — first 30 min

data source. Cache in Redis w/ 30s TTL.
