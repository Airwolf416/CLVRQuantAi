Spot vs Perp Spread:
spreadPct = ((perpPrice - spotPrice) / spotPrice) * 100
> +0.5% вҶ’ PERP_PREMIUM (futures more bullish than spot)
< -0.5% вҶ’ PERP_DISCOUNT (futures more bearish than spot)
Only show spread if both spot AND perp exist with real prices

CLVR Market Mode Score (0-100, deterministic weighted formula):
score = 50
+ (btcFunding < -0.0005 ? +12 : btcFunding > 0.001 ? -15 : 0)
+ btcChange * 3.5
+ ethChange * 2.0
+ nvdaChange * 2.5
+ spyChange * 2.0
- goldChange * 2.0   вҶҗ gold up = risk-off
+ oilChange * 0.8
+ vixScore           вҶҗ VIX < 16 вҶ’ +18, VIX > 30 вҶ’ -24
+ (sentimentScore - 50) * 0.3
score >= 60 вҶ’ RISK-ON | score <= 40 вҶ’ RISK-OFF | else вҶ’ NEUTRAL
Same inputs = same output always (deterministic, no randomness)
